E-R diagram is a simple way to model database structure representing the overall logical structure of database graphically.  The rectangle (entities) represents the things about which data is stored. In the figure it represents stored data of students and clubs. The oval(attributes) are the special datas collected for every entity. In the figure it has tracked data of students like name and id of students sand for club it tracked the id, name ,mentor of club. The relationship(diamond) it represented the membership which has connected students with club.

The number and letter (cardinality) ie: 1 and M indicated the rule of relationship. M:M (Many to Many) shows that a student can be a member of multiple clubs and also a club can hold multiple students.
